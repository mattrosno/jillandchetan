The Groom
Chetan Prakash Huded
Jill + Chetan

We started dating at the end of our first year at Dartmouth. Richard Cheng almost tried to thwart Chetan's plans of asking me out (apparently he was afraid Chetan would get shot down). Luckily Chetan didn't listen to his advice, and asked me for a walk around Occum Pond. Actually, he first asked me if I wanted to shoot hoops which I politely declined, and the rest is history. I've learned about the Chicago Bears while Chetan has grown fond of the Huskers (imagine my shock when I realized he didn't know who Tom Osborne was)!

Proposal

Chetan and our friends Andy and Ami schemed our proposal over Chicago deep dish pizza the spring of 2012, and then they all went ring shopping together. We had planned a trip to Nantucket that fall, and Andy took the responsibility of transporting the ring to the Island. The ring almost disappeared after a travel bag was momentarily forgotten in the taxi that took Andy and Ami to the airport, but it somehow safely made it to Chetan's pocket the night of August 14th on Madaket Beach. He asked me to close my eyes, that he had a surprise. I held out my hands with eyes shut, expecting to receive a trinket from the island. Imagine my surprise when I felt a light, metal band drop into my palm!

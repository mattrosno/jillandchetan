Jill + Chetan

Jill and Chetan started dating their first year of medical school at Dartmouth. They both spent a great deal of time running (when perhaps they should have been studying) and enjoying the beautiful Upper Valley countryside. Once Chetan finally decided he was ready to ask Jill out, their dear friend Richard Cheng almost tried to thwart his plans (apparently he was afraid Chetan would get shot down). Luckily, Chetan didn't listen to his advice and asked Jill to join him on a walk around Occum Pond. This eventually lead to the Couple's Match where they landed two residency spots at Northwestern. They are currently finishing up their last year of residency and loving the vibrant city of Chicago!

Proposal

Chetan schemed the proposal idea over Chicago deep dish pizza the spring of 2012 with their friends Andy and Ami. A trip to Nantucket was already planned for that fall, and Andy took the responsibility of transporting the ring to the Island. The ring almost disappeared after a travel bag was momentarily forgotten in the taxi, but it somehow safely made it to Chetan's pocket the night of August 14th on Madaket Beach. After walking down the beach with a perfect sunset in the background, he asked Jill to sit down and close her eyes. She expected to receive a trinket from the island, and was overjoyed when instead a diamond ring fell into her hands!
